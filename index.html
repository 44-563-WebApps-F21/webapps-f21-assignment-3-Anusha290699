Karimnagar

Karimnagar is famous for its heritage sites such as Gandhi centenary Museum. Located near Lower Manair Dam, on the outskirts of Karimnagar town is a famous tourist attraction called Ujwala Park. Rajeev Gandhi Deer Park located near Ujwala Park, is known for its teeming deer population.It is a very good place to live. Lower Manair Dam is the place where many people visit.I like this place because the people in the town are very friendly.
